MINERS & MINING

Miners & Minings

As a proof-of-work blockchain, BlockDAG's security and long-term sustainability are deeply rooted in the strength and vitality of its mining community.

To foster a robust and engaged network of miners, we've allocated a substantial 75 billion coins (accounting for 50% of the total supply) as mining rewards. This generous allocation underscores our commitment to ensuring that the network remains secure and resilient.

To maintain a stable and attractive cash flow for miners while also supporting the overall stability of the network, we’ve implemented a unique continuous halving model. Unlike traditional halving events that occur at fixed intervals, BlockDAG’s approach gradually reduces mining rewards over a 40-year period. This strategy is designed to provide a predictable and sustainable incentive structure, encouraging long-term participation and dedication from miners. The network supports the SHA256 protocol, and miners from any brand can contribute to the ecosystem, ensuring broad accessibility and participation.

Setting Up Your Rig For Mining BDAG Coins

Setting up your mining rig for BDAG coins involves specific steps tailored to optimize your ASIC miners' performance:

Power Up

Connect your ASIC miners to reliable power supplies. The stability of your setup is critical for efficient mining operations.

Network Connectivity

Link the ASIC miners to your router or switch using Ethernet cables. ASIC miners typically lack Wi-Fi connectivity for enhanced reliability during mining.

IP Assignment

Access your router's interface from a computer within the same network to identify the IPs allocated to each ASIC crypto miner. For streamlined monitoring, consider assigning static local IPs through your router’s settings.

Configuration Access

Enter the identified IP addresses of your ASIC miners into your web browser's address bar. This action should prompt a login page, typically accessible using default credentials provided in the manufacturer's manual.

Pool Configuration

Navigate to the ASIC miner's control panel and access the mining pool configuration page. Follow the guidelines from your chosen mining pool to input the correct Protocol, Address, and Username/Address details to link your miners to the pool.

Ready, Set, Mine

Once configurations are set, your ASIC miner should be primed for mining. Check the miner’s control panel, usually featuring a mining status page to verify readiness.

Rewards Collection

Mining pools will direct your earned BDAG coins to the specified wallet address provided during configuration.

Monitoring Setup

Consider configuring an external computer to act as a monitoring tool for your miners. Numerous solutions exist, so it`s beneficial to join a mining community to select a widely used monitoring tool for efficient oversight.

By meticulously setting up your ASIC crypto miners and establishing a monitoring system, you can optimise mining efficiency and maximise your BlockDAG coin rewards.
