How To Buy BlockDAG Coins

To initiate buying BDAG coins, follow the steps explained in the guide below. Please note that the buying process concerning different payment methods is further explained, so read them carefully before getting started.

Buying CoinsBuying MinersDownloading X1 Mining App
HOW TO BUY

Step 1

To begin, make sure you have a MetaMask wallet installed on your browser, or use one of the wallets supported by Wallet Connect (we recommend Trust Wallet or Plus Wallet).

Purchasing on a desktop browser will give you a smoother purchasing experience. For this we recommend Metamask.

If you are purchasing on mobile, we recommend using Trust Wallet or Plus Wallet and connecting through built in Wallet Connect.

HOW TO BUY

Step 2

Once you have your preferred wallet provider ready, click “Connect Wallet” and select the appropriate option.
For mobile wallet apps you will need to select “Wallet Connect”

Buy ETH, BNB & USDT with Apple Pay, Google Pay or Card

  • Visit  https://www.moonpay.com/buy to purchase ETH, BNB, or USDT.

  • The purchased cryptocurrency will be sent directly to your wallet.

  • You can then use this ETH, BNB, or USDT to purchase BDAG Coins.

Buy BlockDAG with ETH or BNB

  • Ensure you have sufficient ETH or BNB in your wallet (if not, refer to option 1).

  • You can now swap your ETH or BNB for BlockDAG Network.

  • Enter the amount of BlockDAG Network you wish to purchase (minimum $15).

  • Click “Buy with ETH” or “Buy with BNB.”

  • Your wallet provider will prompt you to confirm the transaction and display the gas fee.

Buy BlockDAG with USDT

  • Ensure you have at least $20 of USDT in your wallet before starting the transaction.

  • Enter the amount of BlockDAG Network you wish to purchase (minimum $15).

  • Click “Convert USDT.”

  • You will be prompted to approve the purchase twice:

  • First approval is for the USDT contract.

  • Second approval is for the transaction amount.

  • Make sure to complete both approval steps to finalize the transaction.

This format organizes the steps for easier understanding.

Buy BlockDAG with Selected Crypto

After selecting your preferred cryptocurrency (e.g., BTC, USDT, KAS, DOGE), you will receive a unique wallet address along with the amount to be paid and your order number.

  • Send the specified amount of the selected cryptocurrency to the provided wallet address.

  • Use the QR code for quick and easy payment via your wallet app.

  • This wallet address is valid for a single transaction only; do not reuse it.

  • Ensure you complete the payment within the provided time limit (20 minutes).

  • Once the transaction is confirmed, your BDAG coins will be credited to your account.

HOW TO BUY

Step 3

Once the presale has concluded, you will be airdropped your BlockDAG coins. The exact date and time of the airdrop will be revealed closer to the time of the BDAG launch.Buy
